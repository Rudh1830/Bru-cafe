BRU CAFE

BRU Instant Coffee was launched in 1968 and was India’s first coffee- chicory mix instant coffee. It has remained India’s No. 1 coffee brand since then. It is made from a fine blend of the choicest plantation and Robusta beans. These coffee beans are first roasted to perfection then our processes ensure that the fresh coffee aroma is preserved so that you get a rich coffee taste beyond compare. Bru Instant coffee can be used to make an aromatic coffee.
